How to bring Adeni Tea

For Arabic click here

Welcome to this small recipe. In this recipe I'll try to learn you how you can bring cool Adeni tea, The best tea in the world!, that referce to the culture of Yemen

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Hail
  • Mint
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Boil water for 20 minutes.
  2. Add the sugar, then let it boil for another ten minutes.
  3. Add the tea leaves and let it boil for another 15 minutes.
  4. Then add the mint leaves and milk until it boils, then serve and enjoy 😍
